Running offers significant mental health benefits beyond physical fitness, including reduced stress, eased anxiety and depression, improved sleep quality, and boosted mood and mental clarity. When you run, your body releases endorphins and serotonin while lowering stress hormones, creating both immediate and long-term psychological improvements, according to Hinge Health physical therapist Kelsey Ryks, PT, DPT.
