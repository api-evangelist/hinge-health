This article explains gracilis muscle pain, which affects the inner thigh adductor muscle running from the pelvis to the knee. Common causes include muscle strains, repetitive stress injuries, and sudden increases in activity; most cases improve well with gradual movement and gentle exercises rather than complete rest, according to physical therapist Scott Loving.
